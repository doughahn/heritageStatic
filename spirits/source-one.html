Source One Single Estate Vodka, Rested in Oak Sherry Casks

This single-estate vodka is distilled from grains grown on on land in Nevada and rested in Oloroso Oak Sherry Casks.

  • Twice-distilled.
  • Rich color designates this as a unique vodka.
  • Source One's water comes from the historic Well One, fed by the high Sierra snowmelt and dedicated to our distillery.
  • Rested over 20 days in Spanish Oloroso Oak Sherry Casks.

Tasting Notes

Floral aromas lead to ripe cherry and raspberry on the palate. The creamy oat mouthfeel produces a lovely finish for this unique expression of Source One.

750 ml; 40% ALC/VOL

It's like nothing else I've ever tasted. It's got everything the nose promises, and more. It's nutty, slightly vanilla sweet, floral, peppery and spicy, with fresh fruit too, like red berries and dark cherries. It's got a wonderful creamy mouthfeel, and a satisfying sherry-like finish.

— Travel Distilled

Our vodka is made from oats and wheat that were exclusively grown on the Bently rown on land estate. We ferment with a neutral yeast, distill once through our American column still, and then again through the 40 rectification trays on our Carl German pot still. This achieves 95-96% alcohol by volume. Finally, it’s rested in Oloroso Oak Sherry Casks for over twenty days. Oats lend a body and mouthfeel that’s unmatched by any of the other grains we tested during R&D. Sweetness and viscosity come through from the grain, creating a classically clean spirit.
